NIQE pristine model attribution
===============================

The file `niqe_image_params.mat` (keys pop_mu / pop_cov) contains the
pristine multivariate-Gaussian model parameters from the LIVE NIQE Software
Release, redistributed here under its permissive license. It replaces an
earlier, separately-trained model that was found to correlate poorly with
human opinion (LIVE SROCC ~0.54 vs the reference ~0.91).

Source: A. Mittal, R. Soundararajan and A. C. Bovik, "NIQE Software Release",
Laboratory for Image and Video Engineering (LIVE), The University of Texas at
Austin. http://live.ece.utexas.edu/research/quality/niqe_release.zip

Cite:
  A. Mittal, R. Soundararajan and A. C. Bovik, "Making a Completely Blind
  Image Quality Analyzer", IEEE Signal Processing Letters, 2012.
